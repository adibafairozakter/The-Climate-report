# 🌍 The Climate Crisis



A summary of global warming impacts and greenhouse gas emissions based on **UN CC:Learn**.



---



### ⚠️ Impacts of Global Warming

* **Weather:** Increased rainfall & desertification.

* **Environment:** Polar ice loss & rising sea levels.

* **Nature:** Threat to vegetation and fauna.



### 💨 Human Activities & Emissions

Human activities release critical greenhouse gases:

* **Primary Gases:** $CO_2$, Methane ($CH_4$), $N_2O$, and CFCs.



### 📊 Emission Stats (Increases)

* **$CO_2$:** 40%

* **$CH_4$ (Methane):** 150%

* **$N_2O$:** 20%
